TRUMP, PRESIDENT OF THE UNITED STATES, AND TO THE PRESIDENT OF THE SENATE AND THE SPEAKER OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES, AND TO THE SENATE AND HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE UNITED STATES, IN CONGRESS ASSEMBLED, AND TO THE UNITED STATES SECRETARY OF STATE, AND TO THE UNITED STATES DEPARTMENT OF STATE, AND TO THE UNITED STATES DEPARTMENT OF JUSTICE:We, your Memorialists, the Senate and House of Representatives of the State of Washington, in legislative session assembled, respectfully represent and petition as follows:WHEREAS, The people of Iran have repeatedly demonstrated their desire for freedom, dignity, and justice through decades of peaceful protest; andWHEREAS, International human rights organizations have documented arbitrary detention, suppression of peaceful assembly, restrictions on women and girls, targeting of journalists and community leaders, weaponization of medical care, and the use of internet shutdowns to silence dissent; andWHEREAS, Regular Iranians have experienced economic devastation, currency value collapse, reduced access to food and medicine, and more from years of mismanagement and corruption by the Iranian government and broad United States sanctions that have impoverished everyday Iranians; andWHEREAS, Since December 28, 2025, the Islamic Republic of Iran has carried out a violent crackdown of historic proportions in response to public unrest, which credible reports indicate killed at least 7,000 people, with some estimates far higher; andWHEREAS, Iranians are strong, values-driven, and resilient in the face of a fascist theocracy and have organized repeatedly to push for greater rights, prosperity, and representative democracy, including through the Green Movement and the Woman, Life, Freedom Movement and more; andWHEREAS, Iranian Americans and members of the Iranian diaspora in Washington state contribute significantly to our communities and are directly impacted by repression, family separation, travel barriers, and instability; andWHEREAS, United States military intervention risks escalating violence, harming civilians, and undermining the ability of the Iranian people to determine their own political future;NOW, THEREFORE, Your Memorialists respectfully pray that the United States Government:(1) Refrain from any military intervention in Iran and prioritize diplomatic, humanitarian, and human rights-centered strategies that avoid further harm to civilians;(2) Expand coordinated international efforts to support the Iranian people's pursuit of democracy and accountable governance;(3) Increase material and technical support to ensure access to free, secure, and reliable internet and communications technologies;(4) Strengthen targeted sanctions and accountability mechanisms against government individuals and entities credibly implicated in serious human rights violations;(5) Stop or minimize harm to civilians caused by over-broad sanctions that harm average Iranians struggling to survive;(6) Support independent monitoring, preservation of evidence, and appropriate international legal pathways to document and address violations of international human rights law;(7) Immediately halt deportation flights to Iran and protect Iranian nationals in the United States from forced return to conditions of repression or instability;(8) Lift restrictions that bar or unduly limit Iranian nationals from seeking refuge in the United States, including restoring access to asylum and humanitarian protections; and(9) Affirm that the future of Iran must be determined by the Iranian people themselves through a fair, transparent, and internationally supported democratic process conducted free from violence, coercion, or foreign exploitation.BE IT RESOLVED, That copies of this Memorial be immediately transmitted to the Honorable Donald J. Trump, President of the United States, the United States Secretary of State, the United States Department of State, the United States Department of Justice, the President of the United States Senate, the Speaker of the House of Representatives, and each member of Congress from the State of Washington.--- END ---
