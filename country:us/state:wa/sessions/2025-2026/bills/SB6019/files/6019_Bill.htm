(((13)))(12) For the purpose of this section:
(a) "Labor rate" is defined as that portion of the consumer directed employer's hourly rate that is to be used by the consumer directed employer to compensate its workers, including wages, benefits, and any associated taxes.
(b) "Administrative rate" is defined as that portion of the consumer directed employer's hourly rate that is to be used by the consumer directed employer to perform its administrative duties including losses for bad debt, compensation for business and occupation taxes on the labor and administrative rates, and all other costs associated with operating as the consumer directed employer. Funds related to personal protective equipment for individual providers must be considered part of the administrative rate.
(c) "Home care agency administrative rate" has the same meaning as in RCW 74.39A.310.
(d) "Home care agency vendor rate" is defined as the sum of the home care agency labor rate and the home care agency administrative rate.
