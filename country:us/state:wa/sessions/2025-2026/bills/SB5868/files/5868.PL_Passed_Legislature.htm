AN ACT Relating to adding an additional superior court judge in Skagit county and in Yakima county; and amending RCW 2.08.063.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF WASHINGTON:
Sec. 1. RCW 2.08.063 and 2024 c 112 s 1 are each amended to read as follows:
There shall be in the county of Lincoln one judge of the superior court; in the county of Skagit, ((four))five judges of the superior court; in the county of Walla Walla, two judges of the superior court; in the county of Whitman, one judge of the superior court; in the county of Yakima, ((eight))nine judges of the superior court; in the county of Adams, one judge of the superior court; in the county of Whatcom, five judges of the superior court.
