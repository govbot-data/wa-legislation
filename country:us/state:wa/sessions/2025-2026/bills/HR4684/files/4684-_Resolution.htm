HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 2026-4684
HR 4684
ByRepresentative Stearns
WHEREAS, The people of Ireland and the people of Washington state have formed a mutually prosperous relationship from across the Atlantic Ocean; and
WHEREAS, The cities of Seattle and Galway have been sister cities since 1986, engaging in trade, tourism, education exchange programs, and other meaningful projects, which have improved the health and well-being of the residents of both great cities for nearly 40 years; and
WHEREAS, Ireland is known for innovation as a host for technology companies with deep roots in Washington that continue to make advancements in global technologies; and
WHEREAS, The people and economies of Washington state and Ireland thrive due to trade in technology, aerospace, agriculture, and energy, exceeding a total of one billion dollars annually;
NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, That the Washington state House of Representatives recognizes the strong partnership between the people of our state and the people of Ireland.
