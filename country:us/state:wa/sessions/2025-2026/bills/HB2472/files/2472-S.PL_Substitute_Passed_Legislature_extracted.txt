A new section is added to chapter 18.160 RCW to read as follows:(1) The state director of fire protection or their authorized representative must investigate alleged violations of this chapter.(2) Upon the request of a fire code official, a fire protection sprinkler system contractor performing work on a fire protection sprinkler system must produce evidence of a license issued by the director of fire protection under this chapter.
