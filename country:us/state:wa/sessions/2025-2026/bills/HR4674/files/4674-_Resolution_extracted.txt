ï»¿HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 2026-4674HR 4674ByRepresentative EntenmanWHEREAS, The State of Washington supported the creation of Western Governors University (WGU) in 1998 to provide access to affordable education and training opportunities through online, competency-based programs and pledged, along with 18 other member states of Western Governors Association, to take appropriate actions to create policies that support the goals of WGU; andWHEREAS, Fifteen years ago this spring, the State of Washington established an innovative partnership with WGU to create WGU Washington, a first-of-its-kind legislative initiative dedicated to expanding access to affordable higher education for Washington residents through tech-enabled innovation and student-centered design; andWHEREAS, WGU Washington provides high-quality, affordable, and career-aligned degree programs in sectors vital to our state's prosperity, equipping the nurses, educators, business leaders, and technology professionals essential for Washington to thrive in a competitive global marketplace; andWHEREAS, WGU Washington serves as a primary driver toward Washington's educational attainment goal of at least 70 percent of Washington adults aged 25 to 44 achieving a postsecondary credential; andWHEREAS, WGU Washington has achieved remarkable success in serving Washington's adult learners and underserved populations, including our veteran community, by providing high-quality, affordable pathways to degree completion, expanding options for transfer students, and directly addressing the state's most critical workforce needs; andWHEREAS, The impact of this partnership is evidenced by its extraordinary growth, with enrollment surging from 850 students at its inception to more than 13,000 today; andWHEREAS, This collaboration has produced nearly 50,000 graduates, establishing The Evergreen State as home to the third-highest concentration of WGU students and alumni in the nation; andWHEREAS, WGU Washington remains committed to helping Washingtonians achieve their dreams of attaining a credential or degree and career success, and ensuring The Evergreen State remains a great place to live, work, and raise families;NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, That the Washington State House of Representatives congratulates Western Governors University on its 15th anniversary in serving the students of Washington State.
