andWHEREAS, The establishment of innovation campuses is intended to support regional economic growth, attract private investment, encourage workforce development, catalyze technology leadership, and help build a coherent, end-to-end energy strategy to meet future power demands; andWHEREAS, Nuclear energy is a nonemitting, reliable, and resilient source of baseload power that can contribute to energy security, grid stability, and the reduction of carbon emissions while supporting high-wage jobs in manufacturing, engineering, research, and operations; andWHEREAS, Participation in the department of energy's request for information process by states will help ensure that local and regional prioritiesâsuch as workforce training, infrastructure readiness, economic diversification, and technology innovationâare fully considered in federal-state partnerships shaping the future of nuclear energy infrastructure;
