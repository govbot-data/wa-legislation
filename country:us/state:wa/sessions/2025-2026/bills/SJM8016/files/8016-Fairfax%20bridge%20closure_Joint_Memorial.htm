SENATE JOINT MEMORIAL 8016
State of Washington
69th Legislature
2026 Regular Session
BySenators Fortunato, Liias, Dozier, and Nobles
Read first time 02/03/26.Referred to Committee on Transportation.
TO THE HONORABLE DONALD J. TRUMP, PRESIDENT OF THE UNITED STATES, AND TO THE PRESIDENT OF THE SENATE AND THE SPEAKER OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES, AND TO THE SENATE AND HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE UNITED STATES, IN CONGRESS ASSEMBLED, AND THE SECRETARY OF THE UNITED STATES DEPARTMENT OF TRANSPORTATION:
We, your Memorialists, the Senate and House of Representatives of the State of Washington, in legislative session assembled, respectfully represent and petition as follows:
WHEREAS, The Fairfax bridge over the Carbon river on state route number 165 closed permanently in April 2025 due to severe structural deterioration; and
WHEREAS, The Fairfax bridge is on the historic register; and
WHEREAS, The Fairfax bridge provides the sole point of access to a large portion of Mount Rainier national park; and
WHEREAS, The Fairfax bridge provided the sole access between community members south of the bridge and the greater Washington state area; and
WHEREAS, Those citizens living south of the Fairfax bridge must now traverse logging roads over private land in order to access their homes; and
WHEREAS, Many community members now end up spending eight hours or more away from home in order to perform general quality of life actions such as shopping for essentials; and
WHEREAS, These temporary access roads were closed during the recent severe storms, completely cutting off all access to the outside world for residents south of the bridge;
NOW, THEREFORE, Your Memorialists respectfully pray that you determine that the closure of the Fairfax bridge and its immediate replacement be considered an emergency, and that all necessary steps be taken to shift the national environmental protection act review to take place concurrently or after the replacement bridge is erected. We further pray that you recognize the danger represented by the current bridge's condition and that you remove the current bridge from the historic register so that a replacement may be quickly constructed in place.
BE IT RESOLVED, That copies of this Memorial be immediately transmitted to the Honorable Donald J. Trump, President of the United States, the President of the United States Senate, the Speaker of the House of Representatives, each member of Congress from the state of Washington, and the Secretary of the United States Department of Transportation.
